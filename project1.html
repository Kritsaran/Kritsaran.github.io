Project Description: In this project, I selected three images from the public domain and brought them to TextEdit in order to distort and achieve glitch effect. After these images are noticeably glitched, I then transfer them to Photoshop to enhance the glitch looks and to rearrange their forms by using copy and paste command, blending modes, and filters.
