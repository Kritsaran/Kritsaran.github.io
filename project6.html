Composition Project

Premiere Pro

Project Description: In this project, I chopped off and remixed sounds from Jaws and Whiplash movie trailers to create a brand new sound composition that sounds intriguing. I used premiere pro to cut off chunks of interesting sounds from both trailers then repeat, loop, and apply sound effect to create a new narrative out of them.
