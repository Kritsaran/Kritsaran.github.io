Photo Alphabet

Photoshop

Project Description: In this project, I selected old illustrations of imaginary fish from the public domain to recompose them in a form of the English alphabet. I used photoshop to carefully cut out over twenty fish illustrations and rearrange them by bending, stacking, and crossing them to fit each letter's appearance.

English alphabet made out of uncanny fish illustrations
