Poster Design

Illustrator

Photoshop

Project Description: In this project, I designed a poster for a short story titled " The secret life of Walter Mitty ''. I mainly used illustrator to trace out found images and photoshop to adjust the contrast. Composition wise, I center align all text elements to resemble a typical movie poster. When it comes to the meaning, a circle cutout in the middle of his head is there to signify what Walter is daydreaming of.
